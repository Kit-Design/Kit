It also comes as no suprise that there are over 41,415 animals that are on the endangered red list this year.

There is estimated to be around 1-2 million different species of animals in this world.

Some examples of endangered animals are :

Amur Leopard

Amur Leopards have been on the endangered list since 1966, less than 70 estimated individuals alive today, they are being hunted for their fur and there habitat is being destroyed for us to settle in.

Tigers

It is not news that tigers are being hunted, but 3 out of the nine tiger species are already extinct, and most of the others are endangered,especially the Malayan subspecies

Sea Turtles

More specifically, the Hawksbill and the Leatherback turtle. For the past 100 years, the Hawksbill turtle has lost more than 90% of it's population due to humans and other animals eating their eggs and are killed for their flesh and shells.The Leatherback turtle is suffering due to uncontrollable coastal devolpment, examples include :

Extreme Sea level rising and the disturbance of sea turtle nesting/hatching.
